How It Works

Complete workflow from guest arrival to departure and beyond.

Pre-Guest

Guest inquiry arrives. Claude AI responds instantly. Confirms booking. Asks about preferences. Collects dietary requirements. All logged and encrypted.

In-House Guest

Guest requests assistance. Routes to nearest staff. Tracks resolution time. Logs interaction. Suggests upsells intelligently. Resolves disputes with audit trail.

Post-Guest

Guest checks out. System retains history 48 hours. Auto-deletes PII per POPIA. Summarizes stay. Generates feedback report. Prepares return incentives.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this replace staff?

+

No. ORION empowers your team. It handles repetitive inquiries, documentation, and routine tasks so your staff can focus on high-touch guest experiences. Your concierge becomes a strategist, not a receptionist.

I don't trust AI with my guest interactions

+

Fair concern. ORION uses human-in-the-loop design. AI handles first-response and routing. Your staff reviews and approves. Every interaction is logged with complete audit trail. You have total visibility and control.

What about data security?

+

POPIA compliant. Guest data encrypted end-to-end. PII auto-deleted 48 hours after checkout. Operati onal data retained for analytics only. Complete control of your data.
